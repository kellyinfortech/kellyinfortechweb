About Us

Noah Shipping & Logistics Limited is a leading local and international logistics solutions provider for private and public sector players. We support trade and industry through land transport, worldwide air freight, ocean freight, contract logistics, and supply chain solutions. Our full suite of services include: International Freight Forwarding, Cargo Handling, CFS Operations, Clearing and Forwarding, Transportation, Warehousing and Distribution, Insurance and Courier Services.
